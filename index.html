SEQ.01 PHLEBOTOMY // MOBILE

Blood Draw

Professional, sterile venipuncture carried out at your home or chosen location. Sample collection only — ideal if you already have a test kit or your practitioner has arranged your panel. Centrifugation available on request for samples requiring serum or plasma separation.

SEQ.02 FUNCTIONAL // ANALYSIS

Draw & Interpret

Blood draw combined with expert interpretation of your functional test results — assessed against optimal, not just standard, reference ranges. Working with leading labs including FunctionalDX and Genova Diagnostics, this service reveals the nuance behind your numbers and provides meaningful clinical insight.

SEQ.03 NUTRITION // FULL PROTOCOL

Nutritional Therapy

A comprehensive, personalised approach to your health. Beginning with a full initial consultation — covering past medical history, current diet, lifestyle and your goals — you will receive a bespoke protocol encompassing food, targeted supplementation, movement and lifestyle recommendations. Available in person in Bath or online.
