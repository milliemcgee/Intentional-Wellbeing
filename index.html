Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working together

How is holistic coaching different from traditional therapy?
Holistic coaching addresses the whole person - mind, body, and spirit - using multiple modalities like yoga, nutrition, and energy healing alongside traditional coaching methods. It's action-oriented and focuses on creating sustainable change in all areas of your life.
Do I need to have ADHD to work with you?
Not at all! While I specialise in neurodivergent support and have lived ADHD experience, my holistic approach benefits everyone. Whether you're neurotypical or neurodivergent, I meet you exactly where you are.
What can I expect from our first session?
We'll start with a comprehensive assessment of your current situation, goals, and challenges. I'll learn about your unique needs and create a personalised plan combining the most relevant modalities for your transformation.
How long does it typically take to see results?
Many clients notice shifts within the first few sessions, but lasting transformation typically occurs over 3-6 months of consistent work. Everyone's journey is unique, and we'll adjust our approach based on your progress.
Do you offer online sessions?
Yes! I offer both in-person and online sessions to accommodate your needs and location. Energy healing and coaching work beautifully over video calls, and I can guide you through yoga practices remotely.
